# WIX Projects Repository

Welcome to this repository!

This space is dedicated to a couple of projects I created using **WIX**, a platform I discovered a few years ago and quickly came to appreciate. In my view, WIX is one of the easiest and most practical tools for building a website, whether the goal is to launch an idea, create a personal space online, or simply showcase something meaningful in a visually appealing way.

What I like most about WIX is how accessible it is. You do not need to be deeply technical to bring an idea to life, and that makes it a great tool for creativity, experimentation, and personal expression. Sometimes a project becomes a business, and sometimes it simply becomes a memory or a portfolio piece worth keeping. I think both outcomes have value.

In this repository, I have included two WIX-based projects:

## 1. Surf-inspired e-commerce project

This project was created during a period when I was exploring the idea of building an e-commerce website. I wanted the page to reflect a relaxed, surf-style aesthetic, with a focus on chill vibes, lifestyle, and visual identity.

Although the business idea itself did not end up working out the way I had imagined, the project was still a valuable experience. It gave me the opportunity to experiment with branding, structure, and presentation, and in the end, I was left with something creative that I can still share and look back on with pride.

## 2. Personal travel memories project

The second project is much more personal. It is a collection of memories, photos, and moments from different trips I have taken.

This website was created as a way to keep those experiences together in one place and present them in a more engaging format than just leaving pictures stored away in folders. It is simple, personal, and meaningful to me — a small digital space that captures part of my journey.

Feel free to explore and follow along.
